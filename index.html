Built for the way you actually work

Most CRMs make you do data entry instead of deals.

DealDesk is built around the way realtors actually close: a kanban you drag, milestones that auto-populate, contacts that link to deals, and reports that ship to your CPA without a spreadsheet in sight.

What's inside

A real tool, not a spreadsheet with a paint job.

Pipeline kanban

Drag deals from Prospect → Showing → Offer → Under Contract → Pending → Closed. Every card shows contact, property, financials, and days in stage at a glance.

Auto-generated milestones

Every new deal lands with the standard 10-step transaction checklist already built. EMD, inspection, appraisal, financing, closing — tracked with due dates and timestamps.

Contacts + sphere

Lead temperature (hot/warm/cold), source attribution, communication log, last-touch tracking. Tag past clients and never lose a referral relationship.

Listings + showings

Active listings with DOM, price history, showing log, agent feedback. One source of truth for every property in your book.

Documents that stay with the deal

Contracts, disclosures, addenda — all attached to the deal record. Pull them up six months later when the title company calls.

Year-end reports

GCI by month, deals by stage, commission splits, marketing ROI. CPA-ready PDFs with a single click.
